Our Story

About Navika Designs

Navika Designs is a handcrafted jewelry and art studio built from a deep passion for creativity. Each piece is carefully handmade, reflecting tradition, artistry, and individuality.

What began as a small creative passion has grown into a journey of designing meaningful pieces that celebrate beauty, culture, and craftsmanship.

Every design carries a story and a personal touch.

Brand Ideology

“Rooted in tradition, crafted with love.”

At Navika Designs, every creation reflects patience, creativity, and dedication. Our goal is to celebrate traditional craftsmanship while bringing fresh artistic expression to every design.
